About me

My name is Iulia, and I used to be a landscape architect. At least, that was what I formally studied in college and for four short years practiced. Now I am a freelance designer and a front end web developer, based in Vienna, Austria, striving to improve my skillset daily.

I started my career as a front end developer in November 2015 learning the craft of web design/UX, HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. Before that I was involved in many different kinds of activities from which I could not have learnt more. All this diversity helped my find my path and build my confidence that everything can be learned with the right amount of patience. I’m motivated by a lifelong love of learning and I take pride in what I create.

I believe that every single detail matters – structure, proportions, colors, typography, images, patterns, edges… I also believe that one should not reinvent the wheel, but learn how to ask the relevant and the right questions.

When starting a project, I always take my time to plan, sketch and research before kicking off with the final design work. Every project has its target audience with its own needs, taste and expectations.

And, as for the future? Who knows, but I’ll sure be doing web stuff. Maybe for you.
